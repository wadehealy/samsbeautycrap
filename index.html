About Me

I was a male human Jedi Master born on Coruscant during the declining days of the Galactic Republic. Trained by Count Dooku, I was considered a maverick within the Jedi Order, one who did not always follow the Jedi Code; according to my Padawan, Obi-Wan Kenobi, I could have been a member of the Jedi High Council if I had followed the Code. In the years prior to my death, I began studying the secrets of eternal consciousness after death, having long been a student of the Living Force.
